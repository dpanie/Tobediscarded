How to Support Native Pollinators

  • Plant a variety of native flowers that bloom throughout the growing season.
  • Avoid using pesticides, especially systemic insecticides.
  • Provide nesting habitats like bare soil patches, dead wood, and bee hotels.
  • Report sightings of invasive insects like the Spotted Lanternfly or Asian Hornet to local authorities.

Be aware, speak to your community members.
